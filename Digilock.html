DigiLock - A smart bike lock

Rapid prototyping: UX and System Architecture Design

We delivered:

  • A hi-fi Figma Prototype
  • User Stories and Scenarios
  • Use cases
  • Conceptual model
  • Sequence diagrams
  • Activity Diagrams
  • Exequation environment map
  • A database scheme
  • Requirements specification

During 8 weeks, we developed the UX and the overall system architecture design for a digital bike lock. The project was executed together with a team as part of a series of university examinations and received the highest grade.

User Story

Examples

"As the company management, we want to automate the booking of company vehicles so that our processes can run more efficiently."


As a parent I would like to smoothly share access to my bike with my family, so that I can help them with transportation even when I am at a distance“


As someone experiencing bike theft I would like to locate and retrieve my bike in a fast and simple way


Scenario

Example

"The advertising agency Möja has purchased two electric bikes for the company. Gunnar needs to quickly get to their client, 2 kilometers away. He goes to the reception and asks Wayne for the key to one of the bikes. Wayne replies, 'We have new locks on the bikes; just download this DigiLock app, and you'll have access to the company's digital key!' Wayne helps Gunnar download the app and grants him permission to book both bikes during available times. Wayne says, 'There you go, now you don't need to check with me every time you want to ride. Just book a bike on the app and use your phone to unlock it.'"
