RPA-Prototype for University administration

Rapid Prototyping: Business Process Modeling and Agile Project Managment

We delivered:

  • Two Prototypes made in Microsoft Power Automate
  • A process mapping of the institutions administration
  • An implementation plan

On behalf of the company Dizparc and Umeå Universities institution for informatics we developed two prototypes for Robotic Process Automation. The project included mapping the the instituions administrative processes and reviewing their selectability for automation.

During the project we worked agile with the scrum frameworks as a cross-functional team.
